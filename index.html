Why Recycle?

Recycling is one of the most effective ways to reduce waste and protect our environment. Here's why it matters:

Protects Natural Resources

Recycling reduces the need for extracting, refining and processing raw materials, which helps conserve natural resources and protects ecosystems.

Saves Energy

Manufacturing products from recycled materials typically requires less energy than producing them from virgin materials.

Combats Climate Change

Recycling reduces greenhouse gas emissions by decreasing the need for energy-intensive extraction and manufacturing processes.

About EcoLocator

EcoLocator is a student-led initiative that empowers communities to make environmentally conscious decisions with ease. Designed as a smart, interactive platform, EcoLocator helps users locate the nearest recycling centers, understand waste categories, and learn how to dispose of items responsibly.

In a world facing mouting environmental challenges, EcoLocator leverages technology to close the gap between intent and action. Whether you're trying to recycle an old phone or understand the difference between biodegradable and e-waste, EcoLocator provides accurate, real-time guidance.

But EcoLocator goes beyond static maps. It integrates features like:

  • AI-powered waste classification to help identify materials using images,
  • A carbon footprint calculator to raise awareness about consumption habits
